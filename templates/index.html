How is our Milestone Tracker different?

Our tracker is not just a tracker, but contains helpful activities that will help your child hit those milestones. If you are like us, and are frustrated when you go to the doctor's office, fill out those CDC milestone sheets, and then are told that your baby isn't meeting certain developmental stages (with no practical advice on how to help them) then this app is for you.

How it works
Select an age range

Select the age range of your child. Know that this is just an estimate! Each baby is different (mine were!) and you should choose the age range that has the milestones you would like to work on.

Select a milestone

Milestones are divided into categories: physical, intellectual, social and emotional. These milestones come from the CDC list of milestones that a child should be hitting at the appropriate age.

Check off activities

Each milestone has their own set of activities that you can do with your child to help hit that milestone. Activities should be completed many times as they need as this is a big learning experience!
