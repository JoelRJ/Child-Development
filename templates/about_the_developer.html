About the Developer

Hello, my name is Joel

I officially started this project after the birth of our daughter, although I have been thinking about the problem my app solves with my first child. When my son was born and we began taking him to his monthly Doctors visits they would have us go through a checklist of milestones that he should be hitting. Although he would hit some of the milestones, there would often be some that he was not meeting. As a naturally curious person, I wondered if there was a way that we could help our son hit these milestones. I began researching and couldn't find a central location for activities which directly contributed to my sons development in the CDC milestones, so I compiled my own list. This became the Android app you can download today.

Why Head & Heart?

I wanted to build something that would help me as I experienced my children developing emotionally, physically, socially, and intellectually. Head & Heart is the perfect symbol for this. There are many things that we as parents can do to help our children understand the world around them and their place in it. I see much of that development happening in their minds (both physically and mentally) and in their hearts (both physically and emotionally).
